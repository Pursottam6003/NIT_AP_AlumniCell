9th Convocation

NIT Arunachal Pradesh

Dear Alumni, Greetings from NIT Arunachal Pradesh, India!
I hope you are safe and doing well. With the approval from CA, I am immensely glad to inform you that the 9th convocation of National Institute of Technology Arunachal Pradesh, India is scheduled to be held between 27th December 2022 to 29th December 2022 at NIT Arunachal Pradesh, Jote, India for conferring degrees to the students who have passed out in the academic session 2021-22. The exact date, time and venue will be shared with you all by the first week of December 2022.
So, those who are willing to attend, kindly plan accordingly and also do the clearance by this. PhD students must submit a hard copy of the thesis along with clearance by the same.

NOTE: This mail is applicable for those who have completed a successful degree (PhD, MTech, BTech). Hoping for your kind response and consideration. Thanking you
